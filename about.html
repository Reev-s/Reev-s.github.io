About Us

RDS Agri

-Feeding our Food

RDS Agri is a dedicated agricultural distribution company focused on supplying high-quality agricultural inputs to farmers and agri-businesses across South Africa.

We understand that agriculture depends on reliability, timing, and trust. That’s why we work with reputable manufacturers and suppliers to ensure that every product we distribute meets strict quality and regulatory standards. Our mission is simple: deliver dependable solutions that support productivity, sustainability, and long-term growth within the agricultural sector.

At RDS Agri, we are committed to building strong partnerships and contributing meaningfully to the value chain that sustains our nation’s food supply.
RDS Agri was founded with a clear purpose, to strengthen agriculture by providing reliable access to quality inputs and building long-term, trust-based relationships within the industry.

As a chemical engineer, I approach this business with a systems-driven mindset focused on efficiency, structure, and practical problem-solving. Agriculture is a sector that demands precision and consistency, and RDS Agri is built on those principles.

Our goal is not simply to distribute products, but to become a dependable partner to farmers and enterprises in the sector. As we grow, our commitment remains rooted in professionalism, integrity, humility, and service.

Agriculture feeds our communities, our economy, and our future. At RDS Agri, we are proud to play our part.

Thank you for being part of the RDS Agri journey.







Revash Singh | CEO
