About Us

RDS Agri (Pty) Ltd. is a South African agricultural supply company focused on delivering reliable and affordable fertilizer solutions to farmers and agribusinesses.
We partner directly with reputable international manufacturers to source high-quality agricultural inputs, ensuring consistent product performance and dependable supply.

Our Business

RDS Agri specializes in the import and distribution of agricultural-grade urea (46% N) — one of the most widely used nitrogen fertilizers in crop production. Our goal is to build a transparent and efficient supply chain that connects farmers with global producers at competitive prices.
As the business grows, RDS Agri plans to expand into NPK blends, phosphates, and soil nutrition products, strengthening its role as a complete fertilizer solutions provider.

Vision

To connect African farmers with world-class fertilizer products, empowering sustainable growth through affordable and effective plant nutrition.

Mission

To be a trusted partner to the agricultural community by providing high-quality fertilizers, dependable logistics, and transparent business practices that support real productivity on the ground.
