Urea





Urea is a popular and dependable nitrogen fertilizer used by farmers to help crops grow strong and green. It provides a high amount of nitrogen, which is essential for healthy leaf growth and better yields. Urea is easy to handle, dissolves quickly in the soil, and works well on many types of crops. When applied at the right time and rate, it helps improve soil fertility and supports steady, productive crop growth throughout the season.

CAN



Calcium Ammonium Nitrate (CAN) 27% N is a reliable nitrogen fertilizer that gives crops a quick and steady nutrient boost. It contains both ammonium and nitrate nitrogen, so plants can take it up fast and keep feeding over time. The added calcium helps improve soil structure and supports strong, healthy crop growth. CAN is easy to spread, works well in most soil types, and is especially useful for top-dressing cereals, maize, and pasture.

AN (Ammonium Nitrate) 33-34% N





Ammonium Nitrate (AN) 33–34% N is a high-nitrogen fertilizer that delivers fast and reliable nutrition to crops. It contains both nitrate and ammonium nitrogen, giving plants an immediate boost while also providing sustained feeding as the crop grows. AN works well in cooler conditions, spreads evenly, and is especially effective for cereals, grasses, and pasture. When used correctly, it helps drive strong growth and improved yields.

Compound C



Compound C is a granular NPK fertilizer designed for basal application in maize, wheat, barley, sorghum, and other cereal crops. Its formulation delivers a strong nutrient foundation during early crop development, promoting vigorous root growth, healthy shoots, and improved yield potential.

Compound D





Compound D is a granular NPK fertilizer designed for basal application at planting. It delivers a balanced nutrient mix tailored for maize, wheat, sugar beans, and other field crops, especially those tolerant to chlorine. Its formulation promotes early crop establishment and long-term productivity.

DAP



DAP is a premium granular fertilizer offering a high concentration of phosphorus (46%) and nitrogen (18%), making it ideal for basal application across a wide range of crops. It’s especially effective in promoting strong root development and early plant vigor, making it a go-to choice for farmers during planting season.
